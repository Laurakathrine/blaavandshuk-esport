BOOTCAMP

Our bootcamps offer eSports training conditions that are without a doubt among the best in the country. Our bootcamp room has pro-level equipment including the highest-end gaming PCs and Steelseries peripherals. Your stay includes access to the rest of our facility, such as our swimming pool, gym, basketball court, billiard tables and canteen. We offer accomodation in our huts, located 75m from the eSport center.

We offer pre-made bundles for your team, but you can also contact us with any specific requests and we'll make sure to create a tailored offer specific for your needs. Moreover, in case that you are unsure of whether a bootcamp is something for you, we offer the option of working with one of our coaches, who can guide you through a typical day of a professional player and how they allocate their hours in order to make the most out of it. How long you choose to work with him, is entirely up to you. You just let us know!

Loyalty programme

Here at Blåvandshuk Sports Park, we’re loyal and we reward people who choose to visit us repeatedly. If you have attended a bootcamp four times, we offer you a 50% discount on your next visit. Additionally, you will receive a discount on a membership with the Riptides Membership Club, should you wish to be part of it.

Accomodation

Stay in designer cabins surrounded by nature, only 75 meters from the eSports center. The cabins include access to a kitchen and shopping less than 300 meters from the center. Moreover, there are two options you can choose from. There is a hut that can accommodate eight people and a hut that can accommodate four people but has its own bathroom including a toilet and a shower. Both huts cost exactly the same, it’s a matter of preference. You can always contact us if you need additional information, we are more than happy to accomodate your needs.
