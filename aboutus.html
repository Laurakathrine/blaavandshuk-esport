WE'RE REALLY COOL

We are a modern center by the west coast of Jutland in Oksbøl. Surrounded by nature and close to the sea, we offer our guests a unique experience during their stay with us. Since we have been in the business for over 20 years, we are confident that we can offer personalised and high quality service for our customers. Our team is dedicated to giving you an unforgettable experience.

In addition to our eSport centre, our swimming facility offers the highest and longest slides. Our main swimming pool is 27 degrees celsius, 25m deep with a 1m and 3m springboard. Our warmer pool is 32 degrees celsius with waterfalls, slides and more. Additionally our swimming facility offers a jacuzzi for 6 people.

Our main sports hall allows for basketball games, gymnastics, conferences and much more. Our second sports hall offers specialised trampoline equipment
