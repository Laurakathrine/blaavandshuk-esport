The town

Oksbøl is a small town containing a big variety of things to see and experience, including Sportspark Blaavandshuk. Every year, a large amount of tourists from Scandinavia and Germany pays a visit to Oksbøl to experience the breathtaking nature, beaches and the sportscenter. The visitors also have the opportunity to enjoy a beer at the local pub, have dinner at a local restaurant or even go shopping. The local community are very welcoming and more than happy to help visitors.
